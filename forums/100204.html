It's been out for a month I think (correct me if I'm wrong). But I just got my copy of it for the PC and damn, it's probably the best game I've played in a long time. And I haven't even played it for more than 30 minutes.

I'm blown away by it:
- Cyber Commando's
- Robots
- Dragons
- Awesome Weapons
- Really Cool Graphics
- Jokes
- Foul Language
- Good Music
- Futuristic Stuff
- The 80s

Do I dare to say that I've had more fun playing the first ~30 minutes of Blood Dragon than I had in the entire time I played Far Cry 3? Or is that taking it too far?

If you like Far Cry 3, and haven't played Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon, all I can say is that I highly recommend it.
