That works! Thabks!

This is what I got out of it:

$("div#friendlist-header").toggle(function(e){
if(e.target == this){
$("#comcenter-friends").animate({height:80},200);
}
},function(e){
if(e.target == this){
$("#comcenter-friends").animate({height:700},200);
}
});
