Plugin that adds the option to change the background image on battlelog


You can get it here:
http://websiteofandrew.com/static/bf3/background.js

just add that url to your plugins and close and restart battle log. It should then add an option called "change background" to the footer of battlelog next to "change language", click it and it will put a form on the bottom of the page were you can input the url of the image you want to set as the background then click the button and it will change the background of battlelog, permanently. You can change as much as you want, to go back to the regular background just uninstall the plugin.
