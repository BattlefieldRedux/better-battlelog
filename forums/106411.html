Now that BL+ is officially dead, (not sure how long that's been out, but it just came to my attention) I would really, really enjoy if some of the features it offered were carried over to BBlog. My favorite was map-specific loadouts.

Having to change your loadouts between air and infantry maps takes time, and is really a pain. It often ends up in you losing the momentum you need at the beginning of the game.

What would be even better is if I could have clickable, set loadouts that I could Alt+Tab out of the game and set in Battlelog, and then be able to spawn in as that class set-up instantly. I'm not sure if that extent is possible, but any sort of set loadouts would be years better than what it is now.

I realize it's a bit late in the game to be developing for BF3, but at least keep it in mind for BF4, if DICE doesn't hardcode it in this time either.
