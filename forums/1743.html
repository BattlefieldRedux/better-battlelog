wasakakero wrote:
What about minimize the Com Center and center align Battlelog itself?

Not sure if it's known yet, but doing this does exactly that when in full screen (or have a screen wide enough to have the right side com center bar).
---
Make a new favorite in your bookmarks, open up the new favorite and change the url to whats below:
javascript:document.body.className-='fullwidth';void(0);

Now save it, open up battlelog as normal and then click the new favorite and it will instantly force the com center into the small version that's in the corner and center the rest properly.

Here is 2 full screen shots showing it.
Before (after just opening battlelog).
http://i47.tinypic.com/2wrgttw.jpg

After (just after clicking the newly made favorite)
http://i48.tinypic.com/w9tan6.jpg

---
Perhaps you can make it an auto script on BBlog so that as soon as you go to battlelog it runs the above script and changed the layout.
