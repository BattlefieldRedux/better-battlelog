Formerly Ghost Recon Online, Ghost Recon Phantoms launches with a new identity, new features and worldwide distribution.The game is finally out of beta. Which results in both graphic and game play tweaks. The graphics aren't as good as Battlefield 4's, but the game play is. It's all about tactics;
No matter what you fight for, in the heat of battle, you focus on you team, on your tactics. 'Cause when the moment comes, you fight together, or you die alone.


It's a free to play game, with awesome customization abilities and a magnificent game play. So it's worth a try.
