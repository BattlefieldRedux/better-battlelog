Translator
Mirror's Edge is very good, not just for 10 minutes. It isn't the best for the story or the combat, but overall the gameplay is very good and the art direction is excellent. Plus there's the music which personally I like a lot. For $1 is a must have (even for $5).
Medal of Honor is kinda decent, it isn't like Warfighter, I liked the singleplayer story and the multiplayer isn't bad but it lacks content. The SP gameplay is very similar to most of the FPS out there (a lot of corridors, dumb AI, etc.), the MP gameplay is very similar to BC2 but the maps are pretty much small arenas, good for close quarter action. The main game modes are TDM, Domination and Combat Mission, which is very similar to BF's Rush but it isn't based on the MCOMs.
Crysis 2, well it's mainly good for the graphics, hell not even that much (only with some graphics mods it's very good). Compared to the first one the gameplay is a lot more linear and there isn't much you can't do.
