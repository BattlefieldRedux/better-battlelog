The most of you probably already know this song, but I feel the need of sharing it with you guys.
In the lower part of my country, Holland, we have the second week of February a festival named "Carnaval" (Something like carnival). People dress up weird and there will be loads of alcoholic drinks.

The picture below is random taken photo of a small village with "Carnaval".
http://www.rtvoost.nl/images/T3/800x600/23946.jpg


Back to the thread title.. We like other music with this festival, more happier, partier, etc. The biggest hit for "Carnaval" this year is "Het kuikentje piep" (The little chick peep). I just wanted to share this song with you guys, haha. Oh, it's made in multiple languages (English, Portuguese, Spanish, Dutch, Romanian and Catalonian).
