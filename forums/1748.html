Is it not possible to force the script i posted in the other thread to run the 1st time you log into/or open battlelog, and again when you refresh the page?.
---
Make a new favorite in your bookmarks, open up the new favorite and change the url to whats below:
javascript:document.body.className-='fullwidth';void(0);

Now save it, open up battlelog as normal and then click the new favorite and it will instantly force the com center into the small version that's in the corner and center the rest properly.
---
Doing it this way actully keeps the com center minimized when doing anything on battlelog (it works perfectly when you navigate the site), the only time it pops back up is if you refresh the page - at which point you can just hit the script again.
