The last hours I made a great new plugins for german-speaking people.
It displays you the last news from the german fan-page battlefield-3.org.
I hope you enjoy it :)

Battlefield-3.org News feed

http://screen.pkern.at/2012-09-19_2138.png

Plugin:
http://bblog.pkern.at/battlefield-3.org.js


Changelog

Good to know

There's also a simple trick how to display more news. Simply add the amount after the link. Like:
http:// bblog.pkern.at/battlefield-3.org.js?count=4
This link will now show the last 4 news. It's possible up to 14 news.
