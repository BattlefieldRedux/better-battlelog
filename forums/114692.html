Tested the BETA right now for about 2 hours.

Good tutorial about all possible features.
*Very fast gameplay.
*Titan plays like a typical mech-machine.
* In basic no weapon recoil/spread (like COD) - Require less skill, forces fast gameplay.
* Many in-game messages and especially when many titans are on the battlefield it confuses me alot, just shotin randomly on all moving objects.
* For the current average graphics it requires a lot PC power - Stuck on my computer with 1920x1080 on medium settings. BF4 runs fine at high on the same resolution. Maybe a Beta Issue.
* Fresh new mechanics with vertical gameplay because of double jumps, wallruns and a combination of them.
* Pretty much all feels very similar to COD - XP, Menu, Interface, Gameplay.

For me, i don't will buy that game. Just another solid shooter but no highlight for me.
