[SD] SWAT Division (SD) was founded on October 2011 to bring together mature, active and skilled players from around the globe. Over the past months, SD has become a very renowned platoon and major force in the clan scene for Battlefield 3.

Maturity

We’re a mature community with friendly and like-minded players.

Activity

SWAT Division consists of active players by having mandatory and optional practices. The purpose is to improve our teamwork and tactics. We strive for perfection and this can only be achieved by having practices on a regular basis.

High stats

SD currently counts 53 members with an average rank of 65 and 661 SPM. We've an overall K/D ratio of 1.74.

Competition

SD is a member of Fraggednation; a competitive gaming community which offers excellent ladders and tournaments. We play all game modes, but we’re Conquest based.

Awards

SD values each and every member. You’ll be awarded if you show great practice/scrim attendance, or show other qualities such as good conduct, dedication, leadership, teamwork, tactics and performance on and off the battlefield.

Recruitment


Requirements

1. Atleast rank 50
2. 350-400+ SPM
3. Mic to communicate
You have to meet option 1 & 2 & 3 to be accepted.

*Note: If you do not meet these reqs, you can still apply for SWAT Academy. SWAT Academy requires a minimum rank of 40 and 300 SPM.

We're currently recruiting skilled jet pilots, tank drivers and engineers.
