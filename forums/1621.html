Please follow this rules when you report a bug.

1. Search for an already existing report instead of creating a duplicate;
2. Add your BBLog version (See in your Addon Manager);
3. Add your browser (Firefox or Chrome);
4. If helpful, add some screenshots and links that describes your problem more detailed.
