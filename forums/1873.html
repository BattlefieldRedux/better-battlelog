On the Symthic pages, ADS is different per gun (defines how much spread your weapon will have versus firing from hip, in various stances). ADS is affected by various attachments, especially foregrip (which makes ADS worse). These values are accounted for in the BBlog accuracy charts (or I assume they are), but the values themselves are not visible.

Example: ADS spread for normal AKS-74u is 0.4. With heavy barrel, it's 0.1. If you add a heavy barrel on the accuracy plots, the accuracy gets better, but the only way to see the actual ADS value improvement is on the Symthic site itself.

http://symthic.com/comparison.php?wep1=AKS-74u&attc1=None&wep2=AKS-74u&attc2=Heavy+Barrel
