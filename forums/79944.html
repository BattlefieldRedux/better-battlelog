Since the last EA conference, it is certain BF4 will be released in the second fiscal year of 2013 (april 2013 - march 2014) and it is supposed to be a NEXT-GEN title - that also corresponds with the speculations surrounding the release of the next-gen consoles, that should be released at the end of 2013.

Battlefield 4 in facts:

- Running on Frostbite 2 (greatly improved), using it's capabilities on 80%+ (BF3 around 30%)
- It isn't supposed to be a clone of Battlefield 3
- Beta starts late 2013

From what I've heard DICE is ready to start presenting the game, but they will do so around march 2013.
Also, from the internal screenshots, BF4 looks very much like BF3.


Now, it goes without saying that this is purely a speculation and none of this (except the FB2 stuff) has been confirmed by DICE/EA.


So, any information you have - you can share it here with us :)
