* If we have news to publish - For example new releases, important news, etc... - Post it on all relevant social sites of BBLog like Google+, Facebook, Twitter, Reddit, etc...

* Very good english skills
* Smooth and non aggressive in discussions - Always be objective and don't let you disturb by flame and whine posts. Just stand on top of such people :)
* Some experience in community management and PR
* Good experience with most common social sites
* 20+ years old
