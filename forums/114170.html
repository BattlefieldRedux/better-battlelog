I'm apologize if this has already been asked and/or if it's already available and I'm overlooking it.

In BF3 BBL, we can filter the weapons stats list by kit (assault, NG, support, recon) or filter by weapon type (PDW, SGUN, PIST, etc..). I'd love to see something like that for BF4 BBL. To me, that was the single greatest/most useful feature of BBL (with the extended weapons data coming in a close second). I used that feature a lot to quickly determine which weapon was closest to getting a service star.

Filtering by kit or by weapons class should include random pickup weapons matching the kits other exclusive weapon types (even though random pickups are usable by all).

Filter by Kit Examples:
Assault kit: Assault Rifles, M26 variations, Grenade launchers (including M32 MGL), and Defibs
Engineering Kit: PDWs, AT/AA rockets (including HVM-II and M136 CS)
Support Kit: LMGs, XM25 variations, Mortar,
Recon Kit: Sniper Rifles (including AMR variations)

Filtering by weapon class could be just the common weapon classes available to all kits. (i.e. not AR, Not PDW, Not LMG, and not Sniper rifles). Just the Carbine, DMR, Shotgun, Pistol, Grenade, and Knife.

I'm not sure if including DMRs, Shotguns, Carbines, grenade variations, knife variations, and common equipment (c4) for each kit would be best or not...seems like the kit filter would produce a much shorter/more efficient list by including only the items exclusive to that kit (with the exception of random pickup weapons). Maybe it could be a toggle to "only show weapons specific to this kit". Sorry if this is too much info.

BF3 had 8 buttons for this...they way I have it broken down would require 11.

Thoughts? Suggestions?
