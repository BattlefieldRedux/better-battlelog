My new plugin for the german page "scene-gamers.de". It shows the last news in your battlelog.

scene-gamers.de news feed

http://screen.pkern.at/2012-11-21_1137.png

Plugin:
http://bblog.pkern.at/scene-gamers.de.js


Changelog

v1.0 (20.09.2012)
- Release for BBLog v3.0

Good to know

There's also a simple trick how to display more news. Simply add the amount after the link. Like:
http:// bblog.pkern.at/scene-gamers.de.js?count=4
This link will now show the last 4 news. It's possible up to 10 news.
