(Note: currently only supports BF3; updated version for BF4 coming soon)

As I got nearer to completing my weapons unlocks I found it annoying to scroll down to the next weapon with open unlocks.

My plugin adds a button to the weapons filter bar called 'INC' which when clicked hides all completed weapons from the list.
