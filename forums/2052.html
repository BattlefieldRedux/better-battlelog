My new plugin for the english fan-page battlefieldo.com. It shows the last news in your battlelog.

Battlefieldo.com news feed

http://screen.pkern.at/2012-09-20_1831.png

Plugin:
http://bblog.pkern.at/battlefieldo.com.js


Changelog

v1.0 (20.09.2012)
- Release

v1.1 (26.09.2012)
- Reworked the whole script. Optimized server-side caching of the news feed.
- Added auto-reload every 30 minutes.

v1.1.1 (26.09.2012)
- Fixed: Newsfeed was attached twice, because a fail of the timer.

v1.1.2 (30.09.2012)
- Some fixes and code changes.

v2.0 (21.11.2012)
- Complete rewrite for BBLog v3.0

Good to know

There's also a simple trick how to display more news. Simply add the amount after the link. Like:
http:// bblog.pkern.at/battlefieldo.com.js?count=4
This link will now show the last 4 news. It's possible up to 6 news.
