What this plugin do? It integrates a TSViewer into your battlelog sidebar! (It has nothing to do with TSViewer.com!)

http://img.pun1a.net/2012-09-15_1459.png


Configurate the plugin is a bit tricky. If you need help, just ask!

Firstly
, you need the IP/Domain of the TS3 server. (e.g. Player4Fun.de or 91.121.135.26)
Secondly
, you need the TS-Port. You can find it here:
http://img.pun1a.net/2012-09-15_1519.png

after the colon. If there is no colon, you can use 9987 (Default port)!
And thirdly
, you need the Queryport. Default is it 10011. I tested it on 6 teamspeak3 servers and every got this one! But if it's not 10011, you can't find out the port easily. Just ask a Administrator or your hoster!

If you have got all this information it's easy to configure this plugin.
Open the bblog-menu, search this
http://img.pun1a.net/2012-12-26_1216.png

click on Settings and put the information down there.

Plugin-URL:
http://www.skillmonster.de/products/bblog/plugins/TSViewer.js



For people who used the plugin with the personal URL:

just readd the url, remove the old one and configure it again.

How to install the plugin is easy.

1. install BetterBattlelog
2. open your BBLog-option menu:
http://img.pun1a.net/2012-09-17_1728.png

3. click on 'plugins'
http://img.pun1a.net/2012-09-17_1729.png

4. Now it'll be a bit tricky. The easiest way is it to use my generator (link:
http://pun1a.net/bblog_plugins/GenerateTSViewerURL.html
)
5. and then just click on 'add plugin' and paste the link that the generator put out.
