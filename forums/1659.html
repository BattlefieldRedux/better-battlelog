The purpose of this theme is to minimize clutter and get rid of useless stuff + repair some of the bugs from original theme. This is geared towards them peeps who like the original theme (but want it to be somehow better) and already know what each doohickey on the site does (f.ex. know that picture of a handgun means the weapons tab in your stats).

I'm not officially supporting MOWF side of things. If my theme works there it's purely by coincidence. You're free to report those bugs too but I don't guarantee that those will be fixed.
